Lazy Pot Roast Recipe

Description

It's a bunch of meat drenched in Hoisin sauce. What more can I say?

Click here to view the original recipe.

Prep Time    Cook Time    Servings

20 mins       8 hrs        10

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz chopped onion package or 1 medium diced onion
  • 1 5 lb beef chuck roast
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup hoisin sauce
  • 3 medium plums or 6 pitted prunes, halved

Steps:

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Place onion in 5-qt slow cooker.
  3. Oil a large skillet and brown meat over medium-high heat.
  4. Transfer meat to slow cooker.
  5. Combine water and Hoisin sauce, then pour over the meat.
  6. Top the meat with plums and cover the slow cooker with a lid.
  7. Cook the meat for 8-10 hours until tender, and skim the fat if you wish.

Nutritional Facts:

1 serving: 7 oz cooked meat with 1/4 cup Hoisin sauce
