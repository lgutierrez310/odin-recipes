Lazy Shortbread Recipe

Description

This extra buttery, crumbly shortbread recipe will go great with your morning cup of Joe.

Your bread probably won't look as good as this picture though.

Prep Time    Cook Time    Servings

10 mins       30 mins       16 cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup softened unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Confectioner's sugar (optional)

Steps:

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
  3. Add the softened butter and sugar to a bowl.
  4. cream the butter and sugar using a hand mixer until light and fluffy.
  5. Slowly beat in the flour.
  6. Press dough into an ungreased 9-in. square baking pan.
  7. Prick the dough with a fork, then bake for 30-35 minutes.
  8. Once done, cut into squares while still warm.
  9. Cool completely on a wire rack and dust with sugar if desired.
