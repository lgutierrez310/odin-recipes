Lazy Bacon-Wrapped Smokies Recipe

Description

There's no better way to bring your cholesterol sky high than this delectable lazy appetizer full of mouth-watering processed meat.

Click here to view the original recipe.

Prep Time    Cook Time    Servings

20 mins       40 mins       16

Ingredients:

  • 1 14 oz pack of cocktail wieners
  • 1 lb sliced bacon
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar

Steps:

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
  3. Line rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
  4. Wrap each cocktail wiener with bacon and secure with a toothpick.
  5. Place wieners on the baking sheet and sprinkle brown sugar over them.
  6. Bake for 40 minutes then serve.
